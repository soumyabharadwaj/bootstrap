Sneho Yatra @kolkata

Reaching out to rural West Bengal!

ReachOut Kolkata's Sneho Yatra is a bridge - to touch, connect, empower and transform the rural lives in West Bengal. A project that predominantly concentrates on children and their education, it comes as a holistic package - wrought with lessons on digital literacy, communicative English, Science awareness, health and hygiene, life skills and more...

In the pic: Outreach volunteers with the children of Bhadutala Vivekanda High School at Midnapur, 130 kms away from Kolkata
